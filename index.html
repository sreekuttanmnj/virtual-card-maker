How it works

From signup to first swipe.

Five clean steps. No phone calls. No paperwork. No bank account required.

  • Fund your wallet

    Connect a bank or fund the wallet via ACH, wire, or check. Wallet custody holds the balance until you spend it.

  • Choose a card type

    Virtual, employee, vendor, gift, or payroll. Single use, reloadable, or auto expiring.

  • Set the rules

    Amount, merchant, location, time. Mix any combination per card. We check every swipe against them.

  • Push it out

    Send card details by secure email, or push the card straight into Apple Wallet or Google Wallet.

  • Monitor in real time

    Live transaction feed. Approve, freeze, top up, or revoke any card from any device.

Questions

Things people usually ask.

How much does it cost to issue a card?+
No credit check. No setup fees. No monthly minimums. You only need to fund the wallet with the amount you want the cards to spend, and we issue cards on demand from that balance.
How fast can I issue my first card?+
Once your account is verified and your wallet is funded, you can create and send a card in under 60 seconds. Bulk uploads of thousands of cards run in a single pass.
Do you offer physical cards too?+
Yes. Order a physical Visa card with the cardholder's printed name. Cards ship in 5 to 7 business days and inherit the same spend rules as your virtual cards.
What spend controls can I set?+
Per transaction, daily, monthly, and yearly amount limits. Restrict by merchant, merchant category, country, city, day of the week, or time of day. Mix any combination per card.
Can I add cards to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet?+
Yes. Every virtual card can be pushed directly into Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for contactless tap to pay in stores, alongside online use anywhere Visa is accepted.
Is there an API for developers?+
Yes. Our OpenAPI lets you create cards, set restrictions, fetch transactions, and manage wallets programmatically. A sandbox environment is available for testing before you go live.
